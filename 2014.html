The Village Christmas

Photo Gallery

Megaplay '14

The story starts with the life of the students at the college. Just like any and every other college, the freshers are welcomed by the seniors and a bond is developed among the students who come from different walks of life.
It then moves on to show the daily happenings of college life, fun, hurt, anger, and betrayal. It begins to show how we students usually take things for granted without understanding what our parents go through to provide us with such luxuries. The main characters, two village boys try to support their parents paying for their education by working part-time while their friends enjoyed their luxury. We usually believe that everything that happens is centered around us, whereas in reality, it isn't. When the group of friends heads to the village for a project they have to make, they are confronted with the reality of the life that the protagonists live. But they are unable to comprehend anything other than their project and end up being rash and careless.
In an accident due to this rebellious attitude, they end up putting the lives of their friends and their family in jeopardy. When this leads to a scuffle between the friends, the father walks in to teach them a lesson of forgiveness as taught by Jesus. He teaches them a lesson about love, forgiveness, compassion, and understanding all through the life and living of Jesus and how only this will ever give us true peace and joy in our hearts. He shows them the true meaning of Christmas.

Rahul Philip
